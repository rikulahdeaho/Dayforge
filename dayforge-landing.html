FAQ

Common questions.

Is Dayforge really free?
Yes. Dayforge is completely free to use. No paywalls, no hidden tiers — just the full product.
How is this different from Notion or Todoist?
Notion and Todoist are flexible tools. Dayforge is built specifically for the daily rhythm of planning, tracking, and reflecting — without setup or complexity.
What if I miss a day?
That's fine. Dayforge is designed to help you restart easily. Missing a day doesn't break the system.
Does it sync across devices?
Your daily data stays on your device. Optional account is used only for preferences. Sync is planned as an optional feature.
